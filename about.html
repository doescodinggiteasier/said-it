About & Terms

How the game works, and the fine print. Last updated 2026. This page is plain-English; the binding terms are below. Nothing here is legal advice.

Said It? is a game. Some quotes are real and link to their source. The rest are written by Mags (our magpie) in the style of the named person — they are invented, were never said by anyone, and are clearly labeled “made up” at the reveal. Said It? makes no claim that any person actually said a quote shown as made up. The game exists for entertainment and to help people spot fabricated quotes. Think a quote is wrong or harmful? Tap the ⚑ report control on any quote at the reveal, or email us below — we review flagged quotes and remove them quickly.

How Said It? works

Each day you get six quotes per lane — a mix of real ones (verified word-for-word against a cited source) and fakes that Mags invents in the person’s style. Your job is to catch the fakes. At the reveal, every fake is stamped FAKE and Mags owns up to it (“I made this one up”). The fakes are written to be harmless and in-character — never a claim of real-world wrongdoing.

Report & takedown

If you believe a quote is misattributed, harmful, or shouldn’t be there: use the ⚑ Report this quote control on the reveal card, or contact us at hello@saidit.app. We aim to remove credibly-flagged quotes the same day, no questions asked.
